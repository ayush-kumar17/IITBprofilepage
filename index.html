About Me

I'm currently doing my M.Tech in Computer Science at IIT Bombay. It's been a great experience so far — I'm getting to explore advanced CS concepts and also work on some interesting academic projects. I’m especially interested in core subjects like OS, DBMS, TOC, CN, Compilers, and also in emerging areas like Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Big Data. I enjoy figuring out how things work under the hood, and I'm always looking to connect theory with real-world applications.

On the development side, I’ve built projects like a Hotstar clone, a weather forecast app, and a few small web applications. In the AI/ML space, I worked on a skin cancer detection system using deep learning, and did a TV series analysis project involving neural networks and visualizations.

I genuinely enjoy solving problems and building things that work. Whether it's a coding project, a research task, or just exploring a new tech stack — I like the process of learning and improving. I’m always open to new ideas and love collaborating on meaningful and challenging projects.
